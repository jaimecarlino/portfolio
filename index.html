My name is Jaime Carlino. I am currently a graduate student at Cal Poly Humboldt in Arcata, California. I am pursuing a master's degree in Wildlife and an Advanced Geosaptial Certificate. I am broadly interested in the habitat and breeding ecology of wildlife in working landscape, spatial ecology, and plumage polymorphism. My current research on barn owls takes place in the winegrape vineyards of Napa Valley, California ~ A region primarily known for its world-famous wines, and to a lesser extent, for its hundreds of barn owl nest boxes.
